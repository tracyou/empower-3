EMPOWER!

This toolkit is here to support local authorities and - initiatives in meeting their city's energy transision goals.
It aims to align the city strategies with the local initiatives wishes by:

  • Inviting Local Authorities to reflect and to evaluate their own strategies and plans.
  • Structuring the existing information about on-going programs and projects.
  • Providing an overview of what's already happening in the city.
  • Facilitating the setting-up and scaling-up of Local Initiatives, injecting support from the Local Authorities when and where needed
  • Offering advice from the EMPOWER partners.
